AR Quizventure

Try out our AR Quizventure, a cool Instagram filter to make learning fun!

Bored with the usual old-style quizzing? Here's our AR powered instagram filter that brings in a whole new immersive quizzing environment. So, grab your phone and scan the QR Code to try out the filter now!

Instructions: -

1. Scan the QR Code using your smartphone.
2. Tap on the welcome screen.
3. To choose an answer for a question, just tilt your head towards the left or right based on the desired option.
4. Continue the process - while(!end){repeat step-3}.
5. Total score will be displayed at the end of the quiz.
